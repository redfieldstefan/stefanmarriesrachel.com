Activities and Dining

We hope everyone can make the most of their weekend in Central Oregon. Here are a few of our favorite spots!

Outdoor adventures

For outdoor adventures check out the High Desert Museum, Dee Wright Observatory and Lava Fields, the Wizard Falls Fish Hatchery in Camp Sherman, or Suttle Lake for swimming and camping.

Shopping/City Life

Bend is only 30 minutes outside of Sisters and has a lot to offer! Visit the Old Mill district or Downtown for shopping and dining. We love the McMenamin's Old St Francis School for their soaking tubs (this activity may require reservations!).

Good places to eat

Sisters has no shortage of good spots to grab a bite of food or to sit down for a nice dinner, this is a good short list:

  • The Open Door

    Attached to a fine art gallery, the Open Door serves delicious food and wine in a relaxed (but elegant) atmosphere.

  • Boone Dog's Pizza

    Grab a cooked to order woodfire pizzas from this outdoor seating only food cart. Menu rotates regularly based on available ingredients (best Za in town, also good bread)

  • Fika

    Very nice espresso/coffee and light breakfast

  • Sisters Bakery

    Best donuts you have ever eaten. Try the buttermilk bar ;)

  • Angeline's

    Bagels, cookies, coffee and pasteries

  • Sister's Smokehouse

    Come for the hot sandwiches at this butcher shop, maybe stay a minute to check out the wide selection of meats.

  • Three Creeks Brew Pub

    Can't go wrong with pub classics and cold beers brewed on site
